Tournament Rules

Elite FC Mobile Online Tournament

1. Match Settings

  • All matches must be played using official tournament settings
  • Players must not modify gameplay conditions
  • Unauthorized settings may result in match cancellation

2. Scheduling

  • Matches must be played within the scheduled timeframe
  • Players must be ready at match time
  • Failure to appear may result in a forfeit

3. Fair Play

The following actions are strictly prohibited:

  • Cheating, hacking, or exploiting game bugs
  • Intentional match manipulation
  • Account sharing
  • Intentional disconnections

Violations will lead to immediate penalties.

4. Disconnections

  • Early disconnections → Admin decision (rematch or penalty)
  • Repeated disconnections → Possible match loss
  • Admins retain final authority

5. Reporting Results

  • Players must report results immediately after matches
  • Evidence may be required (screenshots/recordings)
  • False reporting may result in penalties

6. Player Conduct

  • Respectful behavior is mandatory
  • No abuse, harassment, or offensive language
  • Unsportsmanlike conduct may result in sanctions

7. Penalties

Admins may apply penalties including:

  • Match loss
  • Point deduction
  • Temporary suspension
  • Permanent disqualification

8. Final Authority

Tournament administrators have full authority over disputes, rule enforcement, and competition decisions. All decisions are final.
